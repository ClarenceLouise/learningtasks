Thai Green Curry

An Easy College Recipe for College Students to create an Authentic Thai Dish!

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 1 Can coconut milk, or low fat coconut milk
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Aubergine
  • 1 Chopped green chilli
  • Thai Green Curry Paste

Methods

  1. To make the curry, cook the paste in a laarge non-stick wok or large frying pan over a low heat, for 5 minutes. Cut the chicken into strips and add to the pan with the chopped onion. Cook for 5-8 minutes ot until no longer pink. Add the aubergine chopped.
  2. Stir in the coconut milk and simmer for about 10 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through. Scatter over the chilli and serve with rice.
